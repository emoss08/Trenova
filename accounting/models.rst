General Ledger Account
------------------

.. py:class:: GeneralLedgerAccount

    Stores general ledger account information for related :model:`organization.Organization`.

    .. py:attribute:: id

        The UUID of the general ledger account.

    .. py:attribute:: is_active

        Designates whether this account is active.

    .. py:attribute:: account_number

        The account number of the general ledger account.

    .. py:attribute:: description

        The description of the general ledger account.

    .. py:attribute:: account_type

        The type of the general ledger account.

    .. py:attribute:: cash_flow_type

        The cash flow type of the general ledger account.

    .. py:attribute:: account_sub_type

        The sub type of the general ledger account.

    .. py:attribute:: account_classification

        The classification of the general ledger account.
